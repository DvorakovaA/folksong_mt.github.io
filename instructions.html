Instructions

In this annotation task, we are interested in the translation of the overall meaning of folk songs' lyrics.
For each word in the original text, decide whether:
  • It is semantically important and is represented in the English translation (Enter)
  • It is semantically important but is incorrectly represented or not represented at all in the English translation (Del/Backspace)
  • It is not important (do nothing; just skip it using the arrow keys)
What is a semantically important word? These are words that you consider essential for the content of the given verse.
Typically these will be autosemantic parts of speech: nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs. You do not have to overthink this.

Finally, rate it with 0 to 5 stars (the more stars, the better the translation in terms of conveying the meaning of the text as a whole).

Don’t hesitate to just use your intuition when the instructions seem ambiguous.
Due to the nature of human languages, there is no way to make instructions that can be directly applied all the time — your judgment as native speakers (familiar with folksongs) is the gold standard here.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • ← / → – move the cursor between words
  • Enter – mark the current word as correctly translated
  • Del / ⌫ – mark the current word as wrongly translated
